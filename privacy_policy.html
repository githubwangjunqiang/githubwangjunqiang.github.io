Privacy Policy for Chinese Zodiac Clock

Effective Date: 2024-07-26

Introduction

Welcome to Chinese Zodiac Clock. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information. Our application is designed to be a completely offline experience.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from our users. All data generated by the application, such as your wallpaper configurations (e.g., style, colors, animation preferences), is stored exclusively on your local device. This data is not accessible to us or any third parties.

2. Permissions Required

Our application requests the following permission solely for its core functionality:

  • android.permission.SET_WALLPAPER: This permission is essential for the application to set the live wallpaper on your device's home screen. We do not use this permission for any other purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

The Chinese Zodiac Clock application **does not integrate with any third-party services** that would collect information from you. This includes analytics, advertising, or social media services.

4. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any data at all, we are in full compliance with children's privacy regulations such as COPPA.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
