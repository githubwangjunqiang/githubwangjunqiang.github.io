Privacy Policy

For Chinese Zodiac Clock
Effective Date: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Chinese Zodiac Clock ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the App.

1. Overview

Chinese Zodiac Clock is a wallpaper and GIF creation application. Some data is stored locally on your device in order to provide core features. In addition, the App uses third-party services such as Google AdMob and Firebase, and those services may collect certain information automatically to deliver ads, measure app performance, and diagnose crashes.

2. Information We Process

We may process information in the following ways:

  • Local app data stored on your device, such as wallpaper preferences, favorites, and app settings.
  • Images you choose for GIF creation or wallpaper customization, which are processed to provide requested features.
  • Technical and diagnostics data collected automatically by third-party services integrated into the App.
We do not require you to create an account to use the App, and we do not intentionally collect personal information directly from you beyond what is necessary for the features you choose to use.

3. Third-Party Services

The App currently uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, approximate location, device information, ad interaction data, and other signals used to serve, personalize, measure, and protect advertising.
  • Firebase Analytics for usage analytics, such as app events, general device information, and interaction data.
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and stability monitoring, which may collect crash logs, device state information, operating system version, and app version information.

These third-party services process data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy and Security

4. Advertising and Consent

The App may display ads through Google AdMob. Depending on your region, you may be shown a notice or consent request related to privacy, ad personalization, or data processing before certain ads are served.

You may also manage some advertising preferences through your Google account or your device settings. For more information, visit: Google Ads Settings .

5. Permissions Used by the App

The App may request or declare the following permissions for feature support:

  • android.permission.SET_WALLPAPER to set wallpapers.
  • android.permission.INTERNET to access online services such as ads and analytics.
  • android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE to detect network availability.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID to support advertising-related functionality where permitted.
  • android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE to access images selected by you on supported Android versions.
  • android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE on older Android versions where saving exported files requires it.

6. How We Use Information

Information processed by the App may be used to:

  • Provide wallpaper, GIF creation, and related app features.
  • Save your preferences and settings locally on your device.
  • Display and measure advertisements.
  • Analyze usage trends and improve app stability and performance.
  • Detect, prevent, or investigate technical issues and abuse.

7. Data Storage and Retention

App preferences and certain created content are stored locally on your device unless a third-party service processes data for advertising, analytics, or crash reporting. Retention periods for data handled by third-party services are governed by those providers' own policies.
