Privacy Policy for Chinese Zodiac Clock

Effective Date: 2026-04-13

Introduction

Welcome to Chinese Zodiac Clock. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from our users directly. All data generated by the application, such as your wallpaper configurations (e.g., style, colors, animation preferences), is stored exclusively on your local device. This data is not accessible to us or any third parties.

2. Permissions Required

Our application requests the following permissions for its core functionality:

  • android.permission.SET_WALLPAPER: This permission is essential for the application to set the live wallpaper on your device's home screen. We do not use this permission for any other purpose.
  • android.permission.INTERNET: This permission is required for loading advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: This permission is required to check network connectivity for advertisement loading.

3. Third-Party Services - Google AdMob

Our application uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data to provide personalized ads. This includes:

  • Device information (such as device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers)
  • Location data (if you have granted location permission)
  • App activity (such as app usage and interactions)

Google AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by following Google's instructions: https://support.google.com/ads/answer/2662922

4. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. However, the application displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google AdMob has its own policies regarding advertising to children. If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child's privacy, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
